Overview
What IIM Is
IIM is a non-executable decision intelligence platform for global mobility. It analyzes
origin-to-destination corridors to surface risk, volatility, and long-term consequences.
What IIM Is Not
l
Not an immigration agency
l
Not a concierge or facilitator
l
Not a law firm
l
Not a marketplace or lead generator
Why IIM Exists
High-stakes family mobility decisions are made using biased, fragmented, or outdated information.
IIM exists to restore clarity and downside awareness.
How It Works
l
Corridor-based risk analysis
l
Volatility scoring and watchlists
l
Cost bands with tail-risk visibility
l
Failure mode mapping
Who It Serves
l
Families planning long-horizon mobility
l
Advisors and institutions seeking neutral intelligence
Revenue Model
Access-based subscriptions and institutional licenses. No commissions or execution fees.
Core Principle
Some decisions cannot be undone. They deserve more than optimism.
