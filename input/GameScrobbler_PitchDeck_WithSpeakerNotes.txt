Game Scrobbler
AI-First Gaming Intelligence Platform
Making gamers’ behavior visible and usable.

Problem
Gaming data is fragmented across platforms.
Developers can’t understand player behavior.
Gamers lack a unified, intelligent profile of themselves.

Why Now
- Cloud gaming, cross-platform play, and live services are booming
- AI-native ecosystems are redefining product expectations
- Timing is perfect to build a behavioral data layer for the gaming era

Solution
Game Scrobbler is a cross-platform behavioral intelligence engine:
- Aggregates and normalizes gameplay data
- Models behavior using our GSP Framework (Gamer Style Profile)
- Generates AI-powered summaries and insights (like GPT for gamers)
- Powers tools for developers, insights for players, and future 
personalization

Traction
- ✅ 1,000+ plugin downloads
- ✅ 200+ daily active users (DAU)
- ✅ Steam login + Playnite plugin live
- ✅ Overwolf integration in progress
- ✅ Early B2B interest from game tools and analytics platforms

Business Model
- Freemium for gamers: access your Neural Profile, share highlights
- B2B SaaS: dashboards, segmentation tools, developer SDK
- API licensing: for aggregators and analytic tools
- Data monetization: privacy-safe, opt-in anonymized insights

Competitive Advantage
- AI-first architecture: not just analytics, behavioral modeling
- Deep engagement metrics vs. surface stats
- Cross-platform from Day 1
- Team with gaming, AI, and infra experience
