GSEAL – Competitive Gaming Trust Layer
- Platform-agnostic identity & verification layer
- Eliminates smurfing, account sharing, fraud
- Multi-signal Seal Score: Play / Peer / Pay
Problem
- No cross-platform identity layer in competitive gaming
- High fraud rates in paid/prize tournaments
- Organizers waste time policing legitimacy
Solution: GSEAL
- Portable Seal Score across all platforms
- Lightweight verification widget (frictionless)
- Three verification signals merged into one score
Target Customers
- Tournament platforms (Challonge, start.gg, etc.)
- Independent organizers
- Competitive players
- Prize & paid-entry ecosystems

Why Now
- Explosion of amateur competitive gaming
- Financial incentives increase fraud
- Platforms lack resources to build verification
Business Model
- Platform licensing based on verified volume
- Organizer subscriptions or per-event fees
- Player Seal+ premium tier
- Payment and prize validation fees
Team

- Ali & Mehrad – Long-time developers, product execution
Vision
- Become the trust layer of competitive gaming
- Standardize portability of verified player identity
- Enable safer, fairer global esports ecosystems
