CO-FOUNDER VALIDATION AGREEMENT
(Time-Bound, Result-Oriented)
1. Parties
Co-Founder A: Ebrahim Eskandari
Co-Founder B: Mojtaba (Arman) Bagheri
2. Purpose
This agreement governs a short, focused collaboration to determine whether a specific venture is worth
pursuing jointly. The collaboration is strictly for validation and decision-making, not execution or scaling.
3. Nature of the Engagement
• Validation-only collaboration
• Progress measured by decisions enabled
• Time-boxed checkpoints
• Stopping early is a successful outcome
4. Scope of Contribution — Co-Founder A
Designs and runs a compressed validation process, conducts conversations, synthesizes signals, and
produces decision artifacts. Not responsible for operations, delivery, sales, staffing, or independent
continuation.
5. Scope of Contribution — Co-Founder B
Acts as strategic partner, provides judgment and context, participates in decisions, and supports access
where relevant.
6. Duration
The collaboration is strictly limited to six (6) months, with no automatic extension.
7. Decision at End of Period
The parties will either explicitly agree to proceed jointly or the collaboration will automatically end.
8. Equity Clause (If Proceeding Jointly)
60% — Mojtaba (Arman) Bagheri
40% — Ebrahim Eskandari
Detailed terms to be defined in a separate agreement.
9. Continuity Rights
If no joint continuation is agreed, Co-Founder B may continue independently. Co-Founder A will not proceed
independently.
10. Living Appendix
A shared website or documentation may reflect current checkpoints and decisions as a living appendix.

11. Good Faith
Both parties agree to act in good faith, with transparency and professional judgment.
