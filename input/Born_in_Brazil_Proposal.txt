Born in Brazil — Feasibility Proposal
Objective
Run a milestone-based feasibility phase to validate Born in Brazil before forming any company or
assigning equity.
Structure
- Monthly stipend: €3,000
- Duration: Up to 36 months, milestone-based
- Milestones:
1) Baby status secured
2) Parents’ residency / naturalization path secured
- Equity discussed once, at end of feasibility only
- Exit protection: full handover, no claim, no competition
